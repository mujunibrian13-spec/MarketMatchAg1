WhatsApp to Buyer Flow

Farmer posts on WhatsApp → AI & n8n process → Buyer receives alert → Buyer responds → Sale recorded.

The Problem We Solve

Smallholder farmers spend hours coordinating sales via WhatsApp, phone, and visits. This leads to delays, lower income, wasted time, and spoilage.

Farmers and cooperatives lack real-time price data and buyer insights, relying on middlemen who pay less.

Cooperatives & NGOs spend heavily on manual coordination, calls, and travel.

Farmers Can Always Post What They Have for Sale

Farmers can send a WhatsApp message anytime describing what they have available.

Example:
“Maize, 500kg, Mbarara”
“Beans, 10 bags, Bushenyi”

The system automatically records and updates availability.

Top buyers are alerted automatically.

How Buyers Receive Alerts

Registered buyers receive instant WhatsApp notifications when produce is posted.

Example alert: “500kg Grade A maize available in Mbarara. Price UGX X/kg. Reply YES to buy.”

Buyers respond directly: YES / COUNTER / PASS.

Sales are logged automatically.
